Privacy Policy

Rank Forge is a static, offline-first web application. It has no backend server, no database, and no user accounts.

What is stored

Your tier lists, uploaded images, and appearance preferences are stored using your browser's local storage, entirely on your own device.

What is never collected

No images, rankings, or personal information are ever transmitted, uploaded, or shared. Rank Forge does not use analytics, cookies, or third-party trackers.

Clearing your data

Clearing your browser's site data for Rank Forge permanently removes everything stored — this cannot be undone by us, since we never had a copy.
